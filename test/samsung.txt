The series consisted initially of smartphones and the first device, the Samsung Galaxy S, was announced in March 2010 
and released for sale in June of that year. Samsung later expanded the Galaxy S line to tablet computers with 
the announcement of the Galaxy Tab S in June 2014 and released the next month. As of April 2020, the latest smartphones 
in the Galaxy S series are the Samsung Galaxy S20, S20+ and S20 Ultra, all released on 6 March 2020, and the latest tablet is the Samsung Galaxy Tab S7 and was released on 21 August 2020.
